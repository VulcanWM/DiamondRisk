You are a very advanced robber. But even advanced robbers have a chance of going to jail.

You have 50 health at the beginning out the game, and the maximum amount of health is 100. You can get health by sleeping.

To win the game, you have to get to as many diamonds as you can before dying or getting caught. But if you get caught by the police, you lose all your diamonds and go to jail, unless you have some kind of vault where you keep your diamond, or have a weapon that will knock the police out.

1

Rob a bank with 1 diamond

Here you have a 1/100 chance of getting caught


5

Rob a bank with 5 diamonds

Here you have a 1/20 chance of getting caught


10

Rob a bank with 10 diamonds

Here you have a 1/10 chance of getting caught


20

Rob a bank with 20 diamonds

Here you have a 1/5 chance of getting caught


50

Rob a bank with 50 diamonds

Here you have a 1/2 chance of getting caught


Sleep

By sleeping, you might get a some health. You will sleep for 5 seconds


Vault

Steal a vault from the bank. If you steal that, all the diamonds you steal successfully, go into your vault. There is a chance of somebody stealing from your vault though.

Here you have a 99/100 chance of losing of getting caught


Weapon

Steal a weapon from the bank. You can get different weapons. If you steal a gun, you will be able to escape the punishment of jail and rescue your diamonds. You can only use the gun once though. The other weapons will be used to fight the people on the road.

Here you have a 96/100 chance of getting caught


Casino: Dice

Pay 5 diamonds, and then get the amount of diamonds what you roll on the dice.


Jobs

Here random people will ask you to get random things for them for diamonds

Here you have a 30/100 chance of getting caught
